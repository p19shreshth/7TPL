# User Guide for 7TPL

## Introduction
Welcome to 7TPL, your AI-driven research companion designed to assist academic researchers and white-collar professionals in performing high-quality research. This guide will help you navigate the features and functionalities of the application.

## Getting Started
1. **Registration and Login**
   - To use 7TPL, you must first create an account. Click on the "Register" button on the landing page and fill in the required information.
   - After registration, log in using your credentials.

2. **Onboarding Questionnaire**
   - Upon your first login, you will be prompted to complete an onboarding questionnaire. This will help tailor the application to your research preferences, including citation styles and research topics.

## Using the Application
### Dashboard
- The dashboard provides an overview of your active projects and recent research queries.
- You can easily navigate between different projects using the sidebar.

### Project Management
- To create a new research project, click on the "Create New Project" button.
- Fill in the project title, description, and relevant research topics.
- Once created, you can access your project from the dashboard.

### Research Queries
- Use the search bar to enter your research queries.
- The application will process your query and provide a summary along with dummy citations generated by the AI model.

### AI Summarization
- The AI summarization feature allows you to quickly obtain concise summaries of your research topics.
- Simply enter your query, and the AI will return a summary and citations for your reference.

## Additional Features
- **User Preferences**: You can update your preferences at any time through the settings menu.
- **Session Management**: Your search history is temporarily stored for your convenience but is not saved permanently.

## Troubleshooting
- If you encounter any issues, please refer to the FAQ section in the documentation or contact support.

## Conclusion
7TPL is designed to streamline your research process, making it easier to manage projects and access information. We hope you find this tool helpful in your academic and professional endeavors.
